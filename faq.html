Frequently Asked Questions

Short, practical answers about our exports, quality, shipping, and documentation.

We are an export company based in Telangana, India, supplying premium agricultural and marine commodities to buyers worldwide. We manage sourcing, quality checks, documentation, and logistics so your cargo reaches safely and on time.
We handle grains and pulses (e.g., rice, wheat, chickpeas), spices and herbs, oilseeds and nuts (e.g., sesame, peanuts), fruits and vegetables, marine products, and processed items. Share your exact specs and we’ll confirm availability and pricing.
MOQ depends on the product and packaging. For most bulk commodities, MOQ starts from 1 x 20’ container. For certain high-volume products we can discuss trial orders. Tell us your requirement and we’ll advise the optimal load plan.
We source from verified suppliers and perform pre-shipment inspections aligned with buyer specs. Third‑party inspection (SGS, BV, Cotecna) can be arranged. We also share photos/videos and lab test reports when required.
From bulk (25/50 kg PP bags) to private‑label retail packs, we customize packaging as per market standards. Moisture‑barrier liners and fumigation/heat treatment are provided where applicable.
We commonly ship under FOB, CFR/CIF. Payment terms include irrevocable LC at sight, TT advance/against documents, and CAD—subject to credit checks and order size. We’ll propose the safest structure for both parties.
Lead time varies by product readiness and destination. Typical timeline: 7–14 days for sourcing and prep, then ocean transit (e.g., 5–7 days to Gulf, 14–25 days to Europe/Africa, 15–30 days to SE Asia). We’ll share an ETA once the booking is confirmed.
Yes. Paid samples can be dispatched by courier. Sample cost may be adjusted in the final order. Provide your receiving address and courier account (if any).
Standard set includes Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin, and Phytosanitary/Health Certificates as needed. Any destination‑specific documents can be arranged on request.
