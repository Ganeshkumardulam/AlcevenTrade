About Alceven International

Alceven International Pvt Ltd is a leading export company based in Telangana, India, specializing in premium agricultural and marine products. With years of experience in international trade, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for quality exports.

Our commitment to excellence, quality assurance, and timely delivery has earned us recognition among global buyers. We maintain the highest standards of quality control and ensure compliance with international export regulations.

From the fertile lands of India to tables around the world, we bridge the gap between quality producers and international markets, fostering global trade relationships built on trust and reliability.
